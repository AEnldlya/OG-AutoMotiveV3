Our Story

Where We
Come From

O.G. Automotive is a family owned and operated repair shop built on one idea: treat people the way you'd want to be treated. No runaround. No fake urgency. No selling parts someone doesn't need.

Our team brings almost 60 years of combined experience across all makes and models — domestic, import, old, and new. Whether it's a routine oil change, a state inspection, or a diagnosis nobody else could figure out, we handle it.

We're a newer shop to the Upper Valley, and we're here to earn your trust one job at a time. When you come in, you're talking to the people doing the work — not a service writer reading from a screen.

We tell customers what's actually going on — even when the answer is "it can wait." That kind of honesty is rare. We intend to keep it that way.

"We fix cars. We don't fix what isn't broken. We don't guess. We look, we diagnose, we tell you straight."

— O.G. Automotive
