Our Story

Where We
Come From

O.G. Automotive started with one idea: treat people the way you'd want to be treated. No runaround. No fake urgency. No selling parts someone doesn't need.

We've been operating out of White River Junction for over 15 years. We've serviced thousands of vehicles — everything from daily commuters and farm trucks to classic restorations. Every job gets the same attention.

We're an independent shop. That means no corporate quotas, no upsell pressures, and no passing you off to someone who doesn't know your car. When you come in, you talk to the people doing the work.

Upper Valley drivers keep coming back because we tell them what's actually going on — even when the answer is "it can wait." That kind of honesty is rare. We intend to keep it that way.

"We fix cars. We don't fix what isn't broken. We don't guess. We look, we diagnose, we tell you straight."

— Mike T., Owner & Lead Technician
