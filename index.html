About the Shop

Built on
Honest Work

O.G. Automotive has been running out of White River Junction for over 15 years. We're not a franchise. We don't have a quota. We fix what's broken, tell you what it costs up front, and get you back on the road.

Our ASE-certified techs have seen everything — from routine oil changes to engines that haven't run in years. We read the codes, dig deeper, and tell you what's actually going on. No upselling. No guesswork.
